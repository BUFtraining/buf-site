Ben

Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Ben found his passion for fitness when he realized the impact it had on him as a person in all aspects of life, especially his mental health.

I had a really difficult early twenties, to the point where I required medication just to function mentally. The fitness lifestyle completely changed my life. I have been medicine free for 5 years now, and have never felt happier or more in control of my life. I'm committed to now give that same feeling to others.
ACE Certified Personal Trainer

Ted

Born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota, Ted discovered his passion for fitness in 2010, working overnight shifts at a local gym while taking morning classes.

Helping clients discover their strength — physically, mentally and emotionally — is what drives me. Seeing someone push past their limits, reach goals, and gain confidence is the most rewarding part of my day. Fitness isn't just a career for me. It's a way to empower people to take control of their lives.
NASM Certified Personal Trainer

Camila

Born and raised in Cali, Colombia, Camila found her passion for fitness after years of battling with her mental health and low self-esteem.

I decided I'd had enough and chose to take control of who I was going to be. I educated myself on everything fitness and health, and have become strong, both physically and mentally, from my journey. Now the most rewarding part of my fitness journey is having the opportunity to share my knowledge with others.
Certified Personal Trainer

Alan

I was drawn to a career in fitness because it's more than a day job for me. I see real change happening in people's lives, and I'm grateful to be a part of that journey for them.
