Who We Are

NYC's Most
Affordable Personal Trainers.No contracts. No catch.

BUF Personal Training was founded with a simple mission: make exceptional personal training accessible to everyone in New York City. Since 2017, we've been delivering high-quality, results-driven training at prices that don't pretend to be a luxury.

Located at 347 W 36th St in Midtown Manhattan, we serve clients from Hell's Kitchen, Hudson Yards, and Chelsea. Our state-of-the-art studio is equipped with everything you need for a real workout.

With over 1,000 clients trained and a 5-star Google rating, we're proud to be one of NYC's most trusted personal training studios. Our certified trainers specialize in 1-on-1 training, semi-private training, virtual sessions, mobility, and diet coaching — every session under $100.
